Patient Call Flow Update

We're Upgrading the
Patient Call Experience

We are launching a unified, smart-routing phone system across Brownsville, Sonora, and Elizabethtown. The goal of this is to cut down on confusion for patients caused by the previous phone flow and wording.

What is Changing?

Unified Network Hours

The phone network stays "Open" continuously from 7:00 AM to 5:00 PM CT (closes at 4:00 PM CT on Wednesdays). The system no longer shuts down during lunch hours.

Priority "Hunting"

When a patient calls a clinic, it rings that clinic first. If staff are busy or away, the call automatically hunts to the other two clinics, ensuring patients are always connected to a live team member.

No "Closed for Lunch"

Patients will no longer hear a recording telling them we are closed during the day. Because of staggered lunch times, calls will seamlessly hunt to a clinic that is currently active.

Self-Service Menu

Patients can now press 4 for refill instructions or 5 for clinic-specific hours, addresses, and fax numbers before ever routing to a receptionist.

Receptionist Action Items

1. The New Patient Call Tree

Patients calling in will now hear one unified greeting, regardless of the clinic they dial, with the following straightforward menu options:

4

Refill Requests

Plays instructions for patient to call their pharmacy.

5

Clinic Information

Plays the physical address, fax number, and hours for the specific clinic they dialed.

Wait / No Input

Patients who stay on the line are then routed to an available receptionist, prioritizing the dialed clinic first, and routing to other open clinics with available receptionists if the dialed clinic is closed or has no available receptionists.

2. Be the DND Master

Because the network no longer shuts down during lunch, the system relies entirely on our receptionists to let it know when you are away from your desk. You must use the Do Not Disturb (DND) button on your physical phone or desktop app.

Site Lunch Hours

Brownsville 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM CT
Sonora & Elizabethtown 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM ET (11:00 AM - 12:00 PM CT)

Important: When you go to lunch, press DND. When you return, turn DND off. If you do not press DND, your phone will ring while you are away!

3. Answering Network "Fallback" Calls

If you are at your desk, you might receive a call intended for a different clinic because their receptionists are busy. To help you answer smoothly, the system adds a Prefix to your Caller ID so you know where the patient intended to call.

[BR] Intended for Brownsville
[SO] Intended for Sonora
[ET] Intended for E-Town

Hopefully, this makes the process easier for patients and our receptionists.
If you have any questions, please have your supervisor reach out to an IT person near you.
